SensIDL - About Us

Project Team

The SensIDL project is mainly driven by the two research institutes FZI Forschungszentrum Informatik am Karlsruher Institut für Technologie and the Institut für Mikro- und Informationstechnik der Hahn-Schickard-Gesellschaft für angewandte Forschung e.V. (Hahn-Schickard) .  Both institutes are both located in the federal state Baden-Württemberg in Germany and member of the Innovations Allianz Baden-Württemberg.
